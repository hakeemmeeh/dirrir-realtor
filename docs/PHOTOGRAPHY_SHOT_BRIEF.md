# DRL Portfolio Shoot — Photographer Brief

**Client:** Dirrir Realtor Limited (DRL), operating under Rabat Properties Limited
**Purpose:** One-day photography shoot to build a signature image library for the DRL website, social channels, and brochure material.
**Deliverable horizon:** High-resolution RAW + JPG set delivered within 7 working days of shoot.

---

## 1. Brand direction in one paragraph

Dirrir Realtor's visual identity is **editorial luxury**: warm neutrals, serif
headlines over calm architectural imagery, warm gold accents, deep navy shadows.
Think _Monocle_ meets _Wallpaper\*_ meets a Knight-Frank brochure. Every image
should feel **aspirational, quiet, and timeless** — not busy lifestyle stock.

Reference aesthetic: [HassConsult developments page](https://www.hassconsult.com/developments),
[Wafi Investment](https://www.wafiinvestment.com/), Iwan Baan and Hufton+Crow
architectural photography.

## 4. Per-property shot list

For **each completed property** we want the following 14 frames (deliver more if
time permits). Use this as a literal checklist on the day.

### Exterior

1. **Hero wide** — full building, 2/3 sky, symmetrical or rule-of-thirds, tilt-shifted verticals. _Non-negotiable; shoot 3–5 variations._
2. **Context wide** — building with street, trees, neighbourhood cue (jacaranda, acacia, gate).
3. **Approach shot** — walking perspective from gate to entrance, leading lines.
4. **Entrance detail** — front door, hardware, house number, landscaping detail.
5. **Dusk exterior** — same angle as hero but shot at blue hour with interior lights on. _This is the "money shot" for the detail-page hero; please reserve golden-hour slot for it._

### Interior living spaces

6. **Living room wide** — camera low, 24mm equiv., one-point or two-point perspective. Styled with at least one soft element (folded throw, coffee-table books).
7. **Living room detail** — one vignette (pendant light, fireplace, art wall, plant).
8. **Kitchen wide** — island / counter as hero, morning light preferred.
9. **Kitchen detail** — hardware, materials, styled countertop (bread, ceramic bowl, linen).
10. **Dining area** — table set with neutral linen + 2 ceramic pieces, warm light.

### Bedrooms + bathrooms

11. **Master bedroom wide** — bed made with crisp white linen + warm throw, curtains gently diffused.
12. **Bathroom wide** — free-standing bath or shower as hero; fixtures styled; one towel + one plant.

### Amenity + feature

13. **Balcony / view** — composition that shows the _neighbourhood context_ from inside the home (jacaranda, city lights, hills).
14. **Amenity** — pool, rooftop, gym, or garden shot with clean composition and ambient light.

## 5. Style direction (hard rules)

### Composition

- **Corrected verticals**. Use tilt-shift lens or shoot wide and keystone-correct in post. No leaning towers.
- **Rule of thirds** for heros; **symmetry** for interior wides.
- **Subject leads**: building/room is the hero. Crop out anything that doesn't support it (traffic cones, cars, loose wires, parked matatus, TV remotes, kettles, personal items).
- **Empty framing**: no people. Movement-in-frame not required.
- **Consistent eye-level**: ~1.3m (standing human POV) for interiors unless a deliberate low angle.

### Light

- **Golden hour only** for exterior heros (first 45 min after sunrise, last 45 min before sunset).
- **Blue hour dusk hero** for each signature property: shoot the same angle 20 min after sunset with all interior lights on — this is the image that will sell the listing.
- **Interiors**: ambient + existing lighting; no harsh flash. Supplemental soft LED panel fill permitted for dark corners. Avoid mixed color temperatures in a single frame.
- **Avoid harsh midday sun** for exterior hero shots — reserve midday for interiors or covered exteriors.

### Color and grade

- Final grade should match DRL's site palette:
  - Warm highlights (orange +10 to +15)
  - Cool-leaning shadows (blue -6 to -10)
  - Slight desaturation overall (-4 to -8)
  - Subtle film grain (Kodak Portra / Fuji Pro 400H feel)
- **Consistent LUT across every final image**. We should be able to place any two images next to each other and feel like one brand.
- Reference grades from the DRL site: navy `#1C2833`, gold `#c5a059`, ivory `#fafaf5`.

### What to avoid

- No wide-angle distortion on faces/objects near frame edges (no 14mm unless deliberate).
- No HDR halos.
- No oversharpening.
- No crushed blacks; preserve shadow detail for dusk shots.
- No heavy Instagram filters (Clarendon, Mayfair etc.).
- No people, pets, vehicles, signage, or brand logos visible (except DRL).
- No flags / banners (unless DRL branded signage is specifically desired for brochure).

## 6. Technical requirements

| Requirement | Spec |
|-------------|------|
| Camera | Full-frame DSLR/mirrorless, **40MP+ sensor** (e.g. Sony A7R-series, Canon R5, Nikon Z7/Z8, Fujifilm GFX). |
| Primary lens | **Tilt-shift 17mm or 24mm** mandatory for exterior + interior wides. |
| Secondary lenses | 24-70mm f/2.8 zoom + 35mm or 50mm prime for detail. |
| Tripod | Leveling tripod with panorama head, mandatory for all heros and interiors. |
| File format | RAW + JPG simultaneous; 14-bit minimum. |
| Color profile | sRGB export; Adobe RGB for brochure master copies. |
| Resolution | **Minimum 6000px on the long edge** for delivered hero files. |
| White balance | Custom / grey card calibrated per lighting scene. |
| Bracketing | 3-stop bracket on every exterior hero for optional HDR blending in post. |

## 7. Post-production standards

Handled by the photographer unless otherwise agreed.

- RAW → Lightroom catalog with applied DRL preset (shared ahead of shoot — ask DRL for the `.xmp` if available).
- **Lens correction, chromatic aberration, vertical/horizontal straightening** on every final.
- **Sky replacement** permitted on exteriors shot in overcast weather (deliver original + edited versions).
- **Removal of:** power lines, pylons, stray cars, cones, trash cans, temporary signage.
- **Perspective correction**: buildings must stand straight. Interior lines must be level.
- **Noise reduction** on high-ISO interiors.
- Final JPGs exported at **quality 85**, long edge 4000px (hero) / 2000px (thumbnail).
